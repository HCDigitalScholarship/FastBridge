ORACLE

With Bridge/Oracle you can discover readable texts and passages in The Bridge Corpus by revealing passages with the highest percentage of familiar vocabulary. Select the text(s) you want to read and the size of the sections you would like to analyze (by number of lines). After you select the the textbooks you’ve used, lists you’ve mastered, and texts that you’ve read, Bridge/Oracle will rank the passages of your text(s) from most to least familiar — and create links to a Bridge/List for each!
